chia and i have been communicating online on a weekly basis since early march 2021. rarely in the same country we have shared snippets of life through our screens, discussing everything from art to relationships to our adventures in nature. online we have had a communion of ideas, laughter and tears –often reaching out to one another via the glass of our devices, simulating the sort of physical contact we may have had had we been in the same room. We have mentored one another, offering advice and encouragement whilst each tending to our practices.

We would like to explore the potential of creating works simultaneously through screen / creating a space without the location barrier. (I often feel as we start chatting we enter in our own time zone, a transparent room with just two of us, a safe space)

Over the period of the residency we plan to open up this forum by creating artworks and interactive pieces that offer an insight into our virtual closeness.
